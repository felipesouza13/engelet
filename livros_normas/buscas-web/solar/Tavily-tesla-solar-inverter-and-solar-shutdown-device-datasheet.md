The Solar Shutdown Device is a Mid-Circuit Interrupter (MCI) and is part of the PV system rapid shutdown (RSD) function in accordance with Article 690 of the applicable NEC. When paired with Tesla Solar Inverter, solar array shutdown is initiated by any loss of AC power.
